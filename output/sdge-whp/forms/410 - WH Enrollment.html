What this form is for

San Diego Gas & Electric Whole Home Enrollment, Form 410. 410 - WH Enrollment is a working form in the SDGE - ESA Whole Home (PP/D) process. It connects to one other form and looks up reference data from one related form.

What happens automatically

Four automated steps watch this form — they run when a saved record changes (two more are currently switched off).

Desktop ReviewDESKTOP_REVI
Runs when a 410 - WH Enrollment record is updated, and only if Submission Status is 'Completed & Ready for Review'.
Sends an email to operations@maromaes.com — subject “SDGE Whole Home Enrollment Review for {the record's Account Name} {the record's Enrollment Number}”.
Enrollment ApprovedENROLLMENT_A
Runs when a 410 - WH Enrollment record is updated, and only if Review Status is 'Enrollment Approved'.
Sends an email to {CreatorEmail} — subject “SDGE Whole Home Enrollment Approved for {the record's Account Name} - {the record's Enrollment Number}”.
Enrollment Corrections RequiredENROLLMENT_C
Runs when a 410 - WH Enrollment record is updated, and only if Review Status is 'Corrections Required'.
Sends an email to {CreatorEmail},{ProjectContactEmail} — subject “Enrollment Requires Corrections {the record's Account Name} - {the record's Enrollment Number}”.
Pending ReviewPENDING_REVI
Runs on a schedule (weekly on Monday), checking 410 - WH Enrollment records, and only if Submission Status is 'Completed & Ready for Review' and Review Status compares to 'None'.
Sends an email to operations@maromaes.com — subject “SDGE WHP Enrollment Pending Review - {the record's Account Name}”.
Currently switched off — it will not run.
Pending CorrectionsPENDING_CORR
Runs on a schedule (weekly on Monday), checking 410 - WH Enrollment records, and only if Review Status is 'Corrections Required'.
Sends an email to {ProjectContact},{CreatorEmail} — subject “SDGE Enrollment Pending Corrections for {the record's Enrollment Number}”.
Currently switched off — it will not run.
Review Notification (Operations)REVIEW_NOTIF
Runs when a 410 - WH Enrollment record is updated, and only if Submission Status compares to 'None' and Reviewer Name compares to 'None'.
Sends a notification.
