What this form is for

Create and Manage all Invoices for SDGE Whole Home Program. Invoice is a working form in the SDGE - ESA Whole Home (PP/D) process. It connects to one other form and looks up reference data from one related form.

What happens automatically

Four automated steps watch this form — they run when a saved record changes (two more are currently switched off).

Final Invoice Pending CorrectionsFINAL_INVOIC
Runs when a Invoice record is updated, and only if Review Status (Coordinator) is 'Pending Corrections' or Review Status (PM) is 'Pending Corrections' or Review Status (Management) is 'Pending Corrections' or Invoice Status compares to 'Pending Corrections'.
Sends an email to {InstallationContractorEmail} — subject “SDGE WH Final Invoice {InstallationInvoiceNumber} Requires Corrections”.
Final Approved by CoordinatorFINAL_APPROV
Runs when a Invoice record is updated, and only if Invoice Status compares to 'Pending Review by PM' or Review Status (Coordinator) compares to 'Approved'.
Sends an email to daccardo@maromaes.com — subject “ACTION REQUIRED | SDGE WH Invoice Project {the record's Enrollment Number}”.
Final Approved for PaymentFINAL_APPROV_2
Runs when a Invoice record is updated, and only if Invoice Status is 'Approved for Final Payment' or Review Status (Management) is 'Approved'.
Sends an email to jblanco@maromaes.com — subject “SDGE WH Invoice {the record's Invoice Number} Approved for Payment”.
Final Payment IssuedFINAL_PAYMEN
Runs when a Invoice record is updated, and only if Issue Date compares to '2025-05-29T07:39:00-07:00'.
Sends an email to {InstallationContractorEmail} — subject “SDGE WH {the record's Invoice Number} Payment Issued”.
Currently switched off — it will not run.
Final Invoice RejectedFINAL_INVOIC_2
Runs when a Invoice record is updated, and only if Review Status (Coordinator) is 'Approved' and Review Status (PM) is 'Rejected' or Review Status (PM) is 'Approved' and Review Status (Management) is 'Rejected'.
No actions are configured.
Currently switched off — it will not run.
Review Notification (Operations)REVIEW_NOTIF_4
Runs when a Invoice record is updated, and only if Invoice Status compares to 'None' and Processor Name compares to 'None'.
Sends an email to notifications@maromaes.com — subject “[Auto-Log] Review Notification | SDGE Invoice - {the record's Enrollment Number}”.
