What this form is for

This form is one of the working forms staff complete as part of Low-Income Weatherization Program. It is organized into 11 sections, covering areas such as Customer Information, Solar Photovoltaic Only, Notification of Deferral of Wx Service, and Solar PV Pre-Qualification. It connects to two other forms and looks up reference data from two related forms.

What happens automatically

Three automated steps watch this form — they run when a saved record changes.

Desktop ReviewSOLAR_DESKTOP_REVIEW
Runs when a Solar Photovoltaic record is updated, and only if Review Status is at least 'Enrollment Approved' and Submission Status is 'Ready for Review'.
Sends an email to operations@maromaes.com; CC: @LastModifier.Email, SubmissionContact — subject “LIWP FWHC | Solar SPV Intake by {the record's Service Provider Name}”.
SPV ApprovedSOLAR_SPV_APPROVED
Runs when a Solar Photovoltaic record is updated, and only if Review Status is 'Enrollment Approved'.
Sends an email to SubmissionContact; CC: operations@maromaes.com — subject “LIWP FWHC | SPV Project Approved for {the record's Enrollment Number}”.
SPV RejectedSOLAR_SPV_REJECTED
Runs when a Solar Photovoltaic record is updated, and only if Review Status is 'Corrections Required'.
Sends an email to SubmissionContact; CC: operations@maromaes.com — subject “LIWP FWHC | SPV Project Requires Corrections {the record's Enrollment Number}”.
