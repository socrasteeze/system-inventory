315 - SoCal WHP Assessment

SCE - ESA Whole Home (PP/D)

What this form is for

Documents and evaluates diagnostic tests performed during a residential home assessment. It includes intake fields for recording results from the Blower Door Test, which measures air leakage and overall envelope tightness; the Duct Test & Sealing, which assesses duct system efficiency and verifies proper sealing; and Natural Gas Appliance Testing, which evaluates the performance, safety, and combustion characteristics of gas-powered appliances. Form 315 ensures accurate data collection to support compliance with program standards and safety protocols. 315 - SoCal WHP Assessment is a working form in the SCE - ESA Whole Home (PP/D) process. It connects to one other form and looks up reference data from one related form.

What happens automatically

Four automated steps watch this form — they run when a saved record changes (one more is currently switched off).

Desktop ReviewDESKTOP_REVI_2
Runs when a 315 - SoCal WHP Assessment record is updated, and only if Submission Status is 'Ready for Review'.
Sends an email to operations@maromaes.com — subject “SoCal Whole Home Assessment Review for {ApplicantFullName} - {the record's ESA Key}”.
ApprovedAPPROVED
Runs when a 315 - SoCal WHP Assessment record is updated, and only if Review Status is 'Assessment Approved'.
Sends an email to {CreatorEmail},{AssessmentContractorEmail} — subject “SoCal Whole Home Assessment Approved for {ApplicantFullName} - {the record's ESA Key}”.
Submission ResultsSUBMISSION_R
Runs when a 315 - SoCal WHP Assessment record is updated, and only if Assessment Status compares to 'Corrections Required' and Reviewer Name compares to 'None'.
Sends an email to {CreatorEmail},{AssessmentContractorEmail} — subject “SoCal Whole Home 315 | Project {the record's Enrollment Number} {the record's Assessment Status}”.
Assessments Pending ReviewASSESSMENTS_
Runs on a schedule (weekly on Monday (limit 1)), checking 315 - SoCal WHP Assessment records, and only if Review Status is at least 'Corrections Required' or Review Status is at least 'Assessment Approved' and Submission Status is 'Ready for Review'.
Sends an email to operations@maromaes.com — subject “SoCal WHP Assessments Pending Review - {the record's Account Name}”.
Currently switched off — it will not run.
Review Notification (Operations)REVIEW_NOTIF_2
Runs when a 315 - SoCal WHP Assessment record is updated, and only if Assessment Status compares to 'None' and Reviewer Name compares to 'None'.
Sends an email to notifications@maromaes.com — subject “[Auto-Log] Review Notification | 315 - {the record's ESA Key}”.
