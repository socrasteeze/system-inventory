210 - Enrollment & Assessment Form

SCE - Building Electrification

What this form is for

Enrollment and Assessment for the Building Electrification Program, Form 210. This form collects essential information for evaluating customer eligibility and assessing the specifications of home appliances, HVAC systems, and other relevant information to determine qualification. It connects to one other form and looks up reference data from one related form.

What happens automatically

Four automated steps watch this form — they run when a saved record changes (two more are currently switched off).

Desktop ReviewDESKTOP_REVI
Runs when a 210 - Enrollment & Assessment Form record is updated, and only if Submission Status is 'Ready for Review' or MAROMA Review Status is 'Pending Review' or Enrollment Status is 'Pending Review'.
Sends an email to {LastModifierEmail} — subject “Building Electrification | Enrollment Review for {the record's Primary Contractor Name} {the record's Enrollment Number}”.
Enrollment ApprovedENROLLMENT_A
Runs when a 210 - Enrollment & Assessment Form record is updated, and only if MAROMA Review Status is 'Enrollment Approved'.
Sends an email to {ContactEmail} — subject “Building Electrification Enrollment Approved for {the record's Primary Contractor Name}”.
Requires CorrectionsREQUIRES_COR
Runs when a 210 - Enrollment & Assessment Form record is updated, and only if MAROMA Review Status is 'Corrections Required'.
Sends an email to {CreatorEmail},{ContactEmail} — subject “BE Enrollment | Requires Corrections for {the record's Enrollment Number}”.
Pending ReviewsPENDING_REVI
Runs on a schedule (weekly on Monday (limit 2)), checking 210 - Enrollment & Assessment Form records, and only if Submission Status is 'Ready for Review' and MAROMA Review Status compares to 'Enrollment Approved'.
Sends an email to operations@maromaes.com — subject “BE Enrollment Pending Review {EnrollmentNumber}”.
Currently switched off — it will not run.
Resubmission for CorrectionsRESUBMISSION
Runs when a 210 - Enrollment & Assessment Form record is updated, and only if Enrollment Status is 'Pending Review' and Submission Date compares to '2025-12-10T14:00:29-08:00'.
Sends an email to operations@maromaes.com — subject “Building Electrification Enrollment Re-Submission by {the record's Primary Contractor Name} - {EnrollmentNumber}”.
Currently switched off — it will not run.
Review Notification (Operations)REVIEW_NOTIF
Runs when a 210 - Enrollment & Assessment Form record is updated, and only if MAROMA Review Status compares to 'None' and Reviewer Name compares to 'None'.
Sends an email to notifications@maromaes.com — subject “[Auto-Log] Review Notification | 210 - {the record's Enrollment Number}”.
