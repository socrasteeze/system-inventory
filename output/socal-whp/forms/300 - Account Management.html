300 - Account Management

SCE - ESA Whole Home (PP/D)

What this form is for

View and manage all Accounts within the ESA Whole Home Program (PP/PD) for Southern California. This form is the central record in SCE - ESA Whole Home (PP/D) — most other forms connect back to it. It connects to six other forms and looks up reference data from eight related forms.

What happens automatically

Seven automated steps watch this form — they run when a saved record changes (three more are currently switched off).

Work Order ApprovedWORK_ORDER_A
Runs when a 300 - Account Management record is updated, and only if Snugg Pro Work Order Checkbox is 'True'.
Sends an email to operations@maromaes.com — subject “Work Order for Project {the record's ESA Key} has been Uploaded on to {the record's Account Full Name}”.
Currently switched off — it will not run.
Customer Interest ReceiptCUSTOMER_INT
Runs when a 300 - Account Management record is updated, and only if Send Email Confirmation Receipt is 'True'.
Sends an email to {AccountEmail} — subject “Whole Home Program”.
Handoff to Installation ContractorHANDOFF_TO_I
Runs when a 300 - Account Management record is updated, and only if Dispatch to Installation Contractor is 'True' and Primary Contractor is at least '06b924d0-99ae-a554-1893-3a12bf2fd6a8'.
Sends an email to {InstallationContractorEmail} — subject “SoCal Whole Home | Installation {the record's Enrollment Number}”.
Contractor Requesting Down PaymentCONTRACTOR_R
Runs when a 300 - Account Management record is updated, and only if DP Invoice Received Date compares to '2025-05-14T07:20:00-07:00' or Down Payment Invoice Number compares to 'None' and Request Down Payment is 'True' and Submit Final Invoice is 'None'.
Sends an email to operations@maromaes.com — subject “ACTION REQUIRED | SoCal Whole Home - {the record's Installation Contractor Name} Down Payment Request”.
Sends an email to {LastModifierEmail} — subject “ACTION REQUIRED | SoCal Whole Home - Down Payment Request Confirmation”.
Contractor Submits Final InvoiceCONTRACTOR_S
Runs when a 300 - Account Management record is updated, and only if Final Invoice Amount compares to 'None' or Final Invoice Number compares to 'None' and Submit Final Invoice is 'True'.
Sends an email to operations@maromaes.com — subject “ACTION REQUIRED | SoCal WH Invoice - {the record's Account Full Name} {the record's Enrollment Number}”.
Sends an email to {LastModifierEmail} — subject “SoCal Whole Home - Final Invoice Submitted {the record's Final Invoice Number}”.
Contractor Submits Enrollment / Audit InvoiceCONTRACTOR_S_2
Runs when a 300 - Account Management record is updated, and only if Invoice Enrollment is 'True' or Invoice Assessment is 'True' and Enrollment Invoice Amount compares to 'None'.
Sends an email to {LastModifierEmail} — subject “SoCal Whole Home - {the record's Enrollment Contractor Name} Enrollment Invoice Confirmation”.
Sends an email to operations@maromaes.com — subject “ACTION REQUIRED | SoCal Whole Home - {the record's Enrollment Contractor Name} Invoice Review”.
Contractor Requesting Down Payment - InitialCONTRACTOR_R_2
Runs when a 300 - Account Management record is updated, and only if Request Down Payment is 'True' and DP Invoice Received Date compares to '2025-05-20T09:36:00-07:00' and Submit Final Invoice is 'None'.
Sends an email to operations@maromaes.com — subject “ACTION REQUIRED | SCE Whole Home - {the record's Installation Contractor Name} Down Payment Request”.
Sends an email to {LastModifierEmail} — subject “SCE Whole Home - Down Payment Request Confirmation”.
Contractor Submits Final Invoice - InitialCONTRACTOR_S_3
Runs when a 300 - Account Management record is updated, and only if Submit Final Invoice is 'True'.
Sends an email to {LastModifierEmail} — subject “SoCal Whole Home - {the record's Installation Contractor Name} Final Invoice Review Confirmation”.
Sends an email to daccardo@maromaes.com — subject “ACTION REQUIRED | SoCal Whole Home - {the record's Installation Contractor Name} Final Invoice Review”.
Currently switched off — it will not run.
Handoff to Assessor ContractorHANDOFF_TO_A
Runs when a 300 - Account Management record is updated, and only if Assessment Status is 'Pending Audit' and Enrollment Status is 'In Progress'.
Sends an email to {AssessorContractorContact} — subject “SoCal Whole Home Program | Eligible to Participate {the record's ESA Key}”.
Currently switched off — it will not run.
Approved for InstallAPPROVED_FOR
Runs when a 300 - Account Management record is updated, and only if Project Stage is 'Installation' and Project Status is 'In Progress' and Assessment Status is 'Approved for Install'.
Sends an email to {PrimaryContractorEmail} — subject “SoCal Whole Home Program | {the record's Enrollment Number} - Approved for Install”.
