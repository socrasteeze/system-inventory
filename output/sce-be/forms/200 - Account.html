What this form is for

View and manage all currently customers within the Building Electrification Program for Southern California. This form is the central record in SCE - Building Electrification — most other forms connect back to it. It connects to four other forms and looks up reference data from three related forms.

One automated step watches this form — it runs when a new record is created (one more is currently switched off).

Customer Interest ReceiptCUSTOMER_INT
Runs when a new 200 - Account record is created, and only if Send Email Confirmation Receipt is 'True'.
Sends an email to {AccountEmail} — subject “Building Electrification Program”.
Approval Notice IssueAPPROVAL_NOT
Runs when a 200 - Account record is updated, and only if Assessment Status is 'Approved for Install'.
Sends an email to {AssignedContractorEmployee.Email} — subject “BE Enrollment Approval Notice {the record's Account Full Name} {the record's Enrollment Number}”.
Currently switched off — it will not run.
