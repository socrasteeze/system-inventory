SCE - ESA Whole Home (PP/D)

What this form is for

Invoice Form for completed SoCal Whole Home Projects that have been submitted for review, approval, and/or payment. It is organized into 12 sections, covering areas such as Final Invoice, Invoice, Enrollment / Home Audit Invoice, and Down Payment. It connects to one other form and looks up reference data from one related form.

Filling it out

There are 175 fields to work with; eight must be filled in, and 22 only appear in certain situations. As you fill it out, 60 fields automatically update, reveal, or check other parts of the form.

Required fields

  • Reviewer (Final) (StatusBy)
  • Review Status (Final) (StatusFinal)
  • Status Date (Final) (StatusDate)
  • Status Date (Enrollment) (EnrollmentStatusDate)
  • Status Date (DP) (DPStatusDateChange)
  • Review Status (DP) (DownpaymentStatus)
  • ESA Key (EsaKey)
