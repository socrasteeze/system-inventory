What this form is for

This form collects pre/post-installation measurements to document the replacement process. It ensures an accurate evaluation of existing conditions and verifies the specifications of the installed equipment, supporting quality assurance and program compliance. 895 - Installation / Warranty is a working form in the Qualified Appliance Replacement process.
