310 - Enrollment Intake

SCE - ESA Whole Home (PP/D)

What this form is for

Documents initial eligibility screening and household information collected at program entry. Includes fields for applicant eligibility verification, household demographics, income documentation, and occupancy status. It is organized into 10 sections, covering areas such as Applicant Information, Eligibility Information, Applicant Declaration, and Demographics. It connects to one other form and looks up reference data from one related form.

What happens automatically

Five automated steps watch this form — they run when a saved record changes (two more are currently switched off).

Desktop Review310_DESKTOP_REVIEW
Runs when a 310 - Enrollment Intake record is updated, and only if Enrollment Status is 'Pending Review'.
Sends an email to operations@maromaes.com — subject “SoCal Whole Home | Enrollment Review for {the record's ESA Key}”.
Enrollment Approved310_ENROLLMENT_APPROVED
Runs when a 310 - Enrollment Intake record is updated, and only if Review Status is 'Enrollment Approved'.
Sends an email to {CreatorEmail} — subject “SoCal Whole Home Enrollment Approved {the record's ESA Key}”.
Enrollment Corrections Required310_ENROLLMENT
Runs when a 310 - Enrollment Intake record is updated, and only if Review Status is 'Corrections Required'.
Sends an email to {CreatorEmail},{ContactEmail} — subject “Enrollment Requires Corrections {the record's Applicant Name (PRINT)} - {the record's ESA Key}”.
Pending Review310_PENDING_REVIEW
Runs on a schedule (weekly on Monday), checking 310 - Enrollment Intake records, and only if Review Status compares to 'Enrollment Approved' and Submission Status is 'Completed & Ready for Review'.
Sends an email to operations@maromaes.com — subject “SoCal WHP Enrollment Pending Review - {the record's Applicant Name (PRINT)}”.
Currently switched off — it will not run.
Resubmission Submitted310_RESUBMISSION
Runs when a 310 - Enrollment Intake record is updated, and only if Resubmission Date compares to '2025-02-27T09:54:00-08:00' and Review Status compares to 'None'.
Sends an email to operations@maromaes.com — subject “SoCal Whole Home Enrollment Resubmitted {the record's Applicant Name (PRINT)} {the record's ESA Key}”.
Currently switched off — it will not run.
Notify Elite Desert Plumbing for Apex Approval310_NOTIFY_ELITE_DESERT
Runs when a 310 - Enrollment Intake record is updated, and only if Review Status compares to 'Approved' or Enrollment Status compares to 'Approved' and Primary Contractor is '90ba06f0-3ef0-a013-6316-3a1bfa9772bf'.
Sends an email to elitedpinc@gmail.com — subject “SoCal Whole Home | Enrollment Approved & Ready for Assessment {the record's ESA Key}”.
Review Notification (Operations)310_REVIEW_NOTIFICATION
Runs when a 310 - Enrollment Intake record is updated, and only if Enrollment Status compares to 'None' and Reviewer Name compares to 'None'.
Sends an email to notifications@maromaes.com — subject “[Auto-Log] Review Notification | 310 - {the record's Enrollment Number}”.
