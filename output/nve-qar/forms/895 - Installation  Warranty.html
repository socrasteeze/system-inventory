What this form is for

This form collects pre/post-installation measurements to document the replacement process. It ensures an accurate evaluation of existing conditions and verifies the specifications of the installed equipment, supporting quality assurance and program compliance. It is organized into 27 sections, covering areas such as Direct Install Measures, Customer Declaration, Refrigerator Replacement, and Customer Information. It connects to one other form.
