Assessment Intake - Form 02

Low-Income Weatherization Program

What this form is for

LIWP FWHC Assessment Intake to evaluate all applicable measures in dwelling. It is organized into 38 sections, covering areas such as Gas Appliance Testing Data, Additional Measures, Infiltration Reduction - Shell Sealing, and Electric Baseload Measures. It connects to two other forms and looks up reference data from two related forms.

What happens automatically

Five automated steps watch this form — they run when a saved record changes.

Sends an email to operations@maromaes.com — subject “LIWP FWHC | Assessment Review for {the record's Enrollment Number}”.
