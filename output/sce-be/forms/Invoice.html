SCE - Building Electrification

What this form is for

Create and process Invoice details for Down Payments & Installations. It is organized into eight sections, covering areas such as Final Invoice, Invoice, Down Payment, and Final Invoice Review. It connects to two other forms and looks up reference data from two related forms.

What happens automatically

Seven automated steps watch this form — most run when a saved record changes (two more are currently switched off).

Invoice Submitted by Contractor - FinalINVOICE_INVOICE_SUBMITTED_BY
Runs when a Invoice record is updated, and only if Invoice Number (Final) compares to 'None'.
Sends an email to bepilot@maromaes.com — subject “Building Electrification | Invoice {the record's Enrollment Number} {the record's Contractor Name}”.
Invoice Submitted by Contractor - Down PaymentINVOICE_INVOICE_SUBMITTED_BY_2
Runs when a new Invoice record is created, and only if Request Down Payment is 'True'.
Sends an email to bepilot@maromaes.com — subject “Building Electrification | Down Payment Invoice {the record's Enrollment Number} {the record's Contractor Name}”.
Approved by Coordinator - Down PaymentINVOICE_APPROVED_BY
Runs when a Invoice record is updated, and only if Invoice Status (DP) is 'Pending Review by PM' and Request Down Payment is 'True'.
Sends an email to bepilot@maromaes.com — subject “BE Down Payment | Invoice {the record's Enrollment Number} - {the record's Contractor Name}”.
Approved by PM - Down PaymentINVOICE_APPROVED_BY_PM_DOWN
Runs when a Invoice record is updated, and only if Invoice Status (DP) is 'Approved for Down Payment' and Request Down Payment is 'True' and Invoice Date (DP) compares to '2025-12-02T08:43:41-08:00'.
Sends an email to jblanco@maromaes.com — subject “BE Down Payment | Invoice {the record's Invoice Number (DP)}”.
Currently switched off — it will not run.
Approved by PM - Final InvoiceINVOICE_APPROVED_BY_PM_FINAL
Runs when a Invoice record is updated, and only if Invoice Status (Final) is 'Pending Final Review' and Assigned User (Final) is 'Roberto Del Real' and Invoice Stage is 'Final'.
Sends an email to rdr@maromaes.com — subject “Building Electrification | Invoice {the record's Invoice Number (Final)}”.
Currently switched off — it will not run.
Corrections Required - Down PaymentINVOICE_CORRECTIONS_REQUIRED
Runs when a Invoice record is updated, and only if Request Down Payment is 'True' and Invoice Status (DP) compares to 'Pending Corrections'.
Sends an email to {ContractorContactEmail} — subject “BE Down Payment | Invoice Requires Corrections {the record's Invoice Number (DP)}”.
Invoice Rejected by Management - FinalINVOICE_INVOICE_REJECTED_BY
Runs when a Invoice record is updated, and only if Invoice Status (DP) is 'Rejected' and Invoice Number (Final) compares to 'None' and Assigned User (Final) is 'Jose Landeros'.
Sends an email to jlanderos@maromaes.com — subject “Building Electrification | Invoice Rejected {the record's Invoice Number (Final)}”.
Corrections Required - FinalINVOICE_CORRECTIONS_REQUIRED_2
Runs when a Invoice record is updated, and only if Invoice Status (Final) compares to 'Pending Corrections'.
Sends an email to {ContractorContactEmail} — subject “Building Electrification | Invoice Correction {the record's Invoice Number (Final)}”.
Review Notification (Operations)INVOICE_REVIEW_NOTIFICATION
Runs when a Invoice record is updated, and only if Invoice Status (Final) compares to 'None' and Processor Name compares to 'None'.
Sends an email to notifications@maromaes.com — subject “[Auto-Log] Review Notification | BE Invoice - {the record's Enrollment Number}”.
