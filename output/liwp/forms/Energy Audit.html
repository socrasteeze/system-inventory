What this form is for

Energy Audit performed within SnuggPro. Energy Audit is a working form in the Low-Income Weatherization Program process. It connects to two other forms and looks up reference data from two related forms.

What happens automatically

Two automated steps watch this form — they run when a saved record changes (one more is currently switched off).

Approved EmailAPPROVED_EMA
Runs when a Energy Audit record is updated, and only if Audit Status is 'Approved'.
Sends an email to operations@maromaes.com — subject “SnuggPro Audit - {the record's Audit Status} {the record's Customer Name} {the record's Enrollment #} {the record's Primary Contractor} Region {the record's Region}”.
Waiver ApprovedWAIVER_APPRO
Runs when a Energy Audit record is updated, and only if Audit Status is 'Waiver'.
Sends an email to operations@maromaes.com — subject “Waiver Needed -SnuggPro Audit - {the record's Audit Status} {the record's Customer Name} {the record's Enrollment #} {the record's Primary Contractor} Region {the record's Region}”.
testTEST
Runs when a Energy Audit record is updated, and only if Audit Status is 'Approved'.
Sends an email to {LastModifierEmail} — subject “TEST {the record's SnuggPro Project #}”.
Currently switched off — it will not run.
