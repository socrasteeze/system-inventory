Enrollment Intake - Form 01

Low-Income Weatherization Program

What this form is for

LIWP FWHC Enrollment Intake Form 01 is used to evaluate farmworker eligibility and participation in the program. It collects essential information, including household utility and energy usage, basic dwelling details, and other supporting documentation. It is organized into 15 sections, covering areas such as Applicant Information, Eligibility Information, Certification of Income & Expenses, and Energy Service Agreement for Rental Property Owner. It connects to one other form and looks up reference data from one related form.
