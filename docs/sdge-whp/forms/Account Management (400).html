Account Management (400)

SDGE - ESA Whole Home (PP/D)

What this form is for

San Diego Gas & Electric's Whole Home Program Account Management. Account Management (400) is the central record — most other forms in SDGE - ESA Whole Home (PP/D) connect back to it. It connects to six other forms and looks up reference data from five related forms.

What happens automatically

Four automated steps watch this form — they run when a saved record changes.

Contractor Submits Final Invoice - InitialCONTRACTOR_S
Runs when a Account Management (400) record is updated, and only if Submit Invoice is 'True'.
Sends an email to operations@maromaes.com — subject “ACTION REQUIRED | SDGE WH {the record's Enrollment Number} Final Invoice Review”.
Sends an email to {LastModifierEmail} — subject “SDGE WH Final Invoice Confirmation {the record's Final Invoice Number}”.
Contractor Submits Invoice RevisionCONTRACTOR_S_2
Runs when a Account Management (400) record is updated, and only if Final Invoice Amount compares to 'None' or Final Invoice Number compares to 'None'.
Sends an email to operations@maromaes.com — subject “ACTION REQUIRED | SDGE WH {the record's Final Invoice Number} Final Invoice Review”.
Customer Interest ReceiptCUSTOMER_INT
Runs when a Account Management (400) record is updated, and only if Send Email Confirmation Receipt is 'True'.
Sends an email to {AccountEmail} — subject “Whole Home Program”.
Approved for InstallAPPROVED_FOR
Runs when a Account Management (400) record is updated, and only if Project Stage is 'Installation' and Project Status is 'In Progress' and Assessment Status is 'Approved for Install'.
Sends an email to {EnrollmentContractorEmail} — subject “SDGE Whole Home Program | {EnrollmentNumber} - Approved for Install”.
